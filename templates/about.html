The Beginning

Born from a Passion for Authentic Indian Textiles

FabricBazaar was founded with one mission: to make genuine Indian fabrics accessible to everyone — from the Kanjivaram sarees of Tamil Nadu to the block-print cottons of Rajasthan.

We built a marketplace where verified manufacturers and artisans can showcase their craft directly to buyers across India and the world, ensuring fair prices and authentic products every time.
